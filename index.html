Jerry (Qilong) Cheng

Jerry (Qilong) Cheng

I am a Research Fellow at New York University’s Biomechatronics and Intelligent Robotics Lab, advised by Prof. Hao Su. Previously, I worked in the Space and Terrestrial Autonomous Robotics Systems (STARS) Lab with Prof. Jonathan Kelly, the Dynamic Graphics Project (DGP) Lab with Prof. Daniel Wigdor, and the University of Toronto Robotics Institute with Prof. Matthew Mackay and Prof. Ali Bereyhi.

Research Interests: learning-based control and vision-driven policies for robots. I design reinforcement learning methods for visual loco-manipulation in bipedal and manipulator systems, with an emphasis on hardware-aware, perception-integrated intelligence for robust, adaptive real-world behavior.
