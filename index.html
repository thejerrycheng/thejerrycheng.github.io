Jerry (Qilong) Cheng

Jerry (Qilong) Cheng 「程启龙」

I am a Research Fellow at New York University, working on humanoid locomotion using reinforcement learning. Previously, I worked in the Space and Terrestrial Autonomous Robotics Systems (STARS) Lab with Prof. Jonathan Kelly, the Dynamic Graphics Project (DGP) Lab with Prof. Daniel Wigdor, and the University of Toronto Robotics Institute with Prof. Matthew Mackay and Prof. Ali Bereyhi.

Research Interests:

My research interests lie in developing unified policies that integrate visual perception, loco-manipulation, and dexterous manipulation for robotic systems. I am also a strong believer that "Those who are truly serious about software should design their own hardware".
